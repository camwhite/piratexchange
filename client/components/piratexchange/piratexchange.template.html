What is pirateXchange?

A matchmaking service between you and the nearest possible user to exchange files.

How do I use it?

Once you enable location you will be automatically added to pool of users. From there you can initiate a search for a match or wait until someone finds you.

How does it work?

Once matched pirateXchange initiates a P2P connection between you and your match.

Can I transfer copyrighted material?

What you send is up to you. However, we suggest sending files you own the rights to.
